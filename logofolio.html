Udghosh

I had an opportunity to design the official logo for annual Sports festival Udghosh, IIT Kanpur. The logo is combination of a horse which stands for speed and passion and the rising man represents the uplifting sportsmanship spirit of the festival. The logo is also a complimentary combination of red and blue which depicts the harmonious existence of fire and ice. Here red stands for passion and energy and ice blue- patience and calmness required for sportsman. The logo construction is based on the golden proportion.

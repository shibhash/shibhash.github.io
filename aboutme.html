HI! there I am Shib, a Fusionist, Artist and Designer.

I hold a BS degree in Physics from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur . My work seeks to push the boundaries of design and technology to create innovative solutions with the power to transform our lives. Some of the projects that I have developed are prototypes spanning from interactive STEM learning tools, frugal innovation in products (prosthesis), simulation software design for scientific research, urban communication and graphic novel. I’m passionate about Product design, Human-Computer Interaction, VR/AR, non-verbal iteration and interactive systems. Moreover, I would love to explore the quite space between virtual, physical and mixed interactive systems to augment human creativity, artistic expression, abstract thinking, storytelling, and learning. I have empathy for the human condition, love for experimenting with technology and a strong aesthetic viewpoint.

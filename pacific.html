Pacific Smoke Version 1.0

Virtual Reality | Game design | Motion Graphics | 3D Animation | VFX | Sound design | Narration

Overview

Pacific smoke

The project explores the possibility of interactive three dimensional environment and virtual reality gaming

Storyboarding

Concept sketches

The story revolves around various islands of Pacific Ocean allied power ship disappearing in a mysterious way. The allied (United States, Britain, Australia) thought of it was Empire of Japan who carried out mysterious experimental weapon.
I was inspired by Pacific battle, World War-II. Game consists of two levels, First level comes with a terrain map with enemy base camps and second level revolves around mystic disappearance of ships in Pacific.





Initial stages

Planning and execution

I sketched few concepts on paper and quickly moved into concept art development and storyboarding. I decided to start with character and game arena design. I faced a lot of problems in setting up camera angle and focus with dummy objects.

I used target cameras to capture the North Kiribati ocean region and the ship.I started experimenting on few more camera angles. Eventually, I came up with an idea of locking the camera focus to the dummy object and camera to a spline.
